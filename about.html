About Loomo

Meet Loomo! A little bookworm who loves to read. The more you read, the better Loomo feels!

Loomo is a reading companion pet designed to help you build a consistent reading habit. You have to read for at least 15min to keep your pet happy.

You can track the books you read, and get insights on your reading habits.

Who Created Loomo?

Loomo is created by Justicia Premo, a solo developer and founder with a love for reading, based in London and Accra, Ghana.
